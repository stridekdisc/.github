# STRIDE

### Transforming Lives Through Inclusive Innovation

**Empowering Communities, Advancing Social Impact, and Fostering Sustainability.**

STRIDE is a Kerala Government / K-DISC initiative building affordable assistive and adaptive technology for Persons with Disabilities through participatory design with students, makers, institutions, and communities.

## About STRIDE

STRIDE moves assistive technology from charity to empowerment by building solutions **with Persons with Disabilities, not just for them**.

We focus on:

* Co-creation
* Open innovation
* Local manufacturing
* Inclusive design
* Affordable assistive technology

---

## Our Ecosystem

* **STRIDE Hub** — Coordination, partnerships, platforms, and strategy.
* **STRIDE Innovation Centres** — Institution-based spaces for prototyping and problem solving.
* **STRIDE Maker Studios** — Local production spaces for building, testing, repairing, and distributing devices.
